The Show

What it is

Two Dads Having a Beer is a podcast built around fatherhood, family, purpose, work, faith, laughter, and the realities dads carry in everyday life.

The Audience

Who it reaches

Dads, families, community-minded listeners, and people who connect with grounded, real-world storytelling over polished hype.

The Fit

What works best

The best partnerships are relevant, useful, local or lifestyle aligned, and genuinely connected to the voice of the show.
