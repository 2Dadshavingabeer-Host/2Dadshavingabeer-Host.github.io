The Show

What it is

Two Dads Having a Beer is a podcast built around fatherhood, family, purpose, work, faith, and honest conversation with dads from different walks of life.

The Audience

Who it reaches

Dads, families, community-minded listeners, and people who connect with grounded, relatable storytelling over polished hype.

The Fit

What works best

The strongest partners are relevant, useful, lifestyle-aligned, local or family-oriented, and genuinely connected to the tone of the show.
