ABOUT THE SHOW

Where the stories matter more than the titles.

Every episode features a different dad from a different walk of life. Some are musicians, business owners, coaches, leaders, and everyday working men. In this space, titles matter less than truth.

Hosted by Brandon Labonte, Two Dads Having a Beer is built on humor, authenticity, and the kind of conversation that feels more like pulling up a chair than tuning into an interview.

“Welcome back to Two Dads Having a Beer — where the only thing more honest than the stories is the drink in our hands.”
