ABOUT THE SHOW

Where the stories matter more than the titles.

Every episode brings on a different dad from a different walk of life. It does not matter what title he holds — in this space, it is about what it really looks like to raise kids, chase purpose, support a family, and stay grounded.

Hosted by Brandon Labonte, the show blends humor, honesty, and meaningful conversation in a way that feels more like sitting down with a friend than tuning into a formal interview.

“Welcome back to Two Dads Having a Beer — the podcast where the only thing more honest than the stories is the beer in our hands.”
