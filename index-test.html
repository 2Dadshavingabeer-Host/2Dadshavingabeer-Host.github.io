MEET THE HOST

I’m Brandon, and this show is built for real conversations dads actually need

Two Dads Having a Beer started from a simple belief: dads need more spaces where they can be honest. Not polished. Not scripted. Just real. The wins, the pressure, the family stuff, the faith stuff, the work stuff, and the lessons we pick up along the way.

I’m Brandon Labonte — husband, father in a blended family, business owner, recruiting leader, and the guy behind the mic each week. This show is my way of creating the kind of conversation I believe more dads are hungry for.

What makes the show different

The host stays constant. The guest dad changes. That means every episode brings a fresh point of view, but the heart of the show stays the same: honest conversation, real life, and a relaxed setting that feels human.

HOW IT WORKS

What being a Guest Dad looks like

Step 1

Fill out the Guest Dad form and tell us a little about who you are, where you’re from, and what kind of story or perspective you’d bring to the show.

Step 2

If it feels like a fit, we’ll line up a recording time. The conversation is relaxed, remote, and built to feel more like a real hangout than an interview.

Step 3

We hop on for about 45 minutes, crack a beer or your drink of choice, and just talk. No script. No pressure. Just real conversation.
